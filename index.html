What Rutera does

Built for one thing.
Bikepacking.

01
Route Atlas
Curated official routes + community uploads. Surface type by segment — tarmac, gravel, singletrack. GPX download, elevation profiles, filters. Spain first, global next.
02
Smart Trip Planner
Enter your start date and pace → see the weather forecast for where you'll actually be each day. Auto-split into stages. Overnight spots snapped to the route.
03
Everything Along the Way
Bivouac spots, mountain huts, Warmshowers hosts, water sources, bike shops, supermarkets with opening hours — one layer, one app, no switching.
Key Features

What no other app does.

🌦️
Route-Aware Weather
Forecast for where you'll be, not where you are now. Enter start date + daily pace → weather at each waypoint on the exact day you'll pass through.
NOBODY DOES THIS
💨
Headwind / Tailwind Analysis
Wind direction vs. your travel bearing, segment by segment. See which stages will be a slog and which will push you forward before you even leave.
NOBODY DOES THIS
🗺️
Surface by Segment
Every kilometer labelled: tarmac, gravel, singletrack, or trail. Filter routes by surface mix. Know what tyres to bring before you pack.
🌙
Overnight Layer
All sleep options in one map layer — bivouac spots with legal status, mountain huts, Warmshowers hosts, bikefriendly guesthouses, campsites.
🛒
Resupply Planner
Supermarkets, bakeries, water sources, bike shops, pharmacies with opening hours. Critical when the next store is 120 km away.
📐
Stage Builder
Set km/day → route splits into stages. Daily effort score weights elevation by surface. Rest days recommended when thresholds are exceeded.
